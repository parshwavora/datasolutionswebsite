CCTV DDR Data Recovery

In this modern age protection has become an important and unavoidable element. Security in the form of CCTV cameras has been installed and used for various purposes from our home to the workplace , schools, offices , shops, banks, parks, traffic signals and even highways.

Specifications

CCTV as a package, along with multiplexers and sensors, consists of cameras, cables, video displays and storage units. CCTV captures monitoring footage, and disk (DAS), network storage area (SAN), or network-connected data (NAS) may be directly attached. Each of them has its benefits, and can be chosen according to consumer requirements.As per the storage solution different interfaces are used. Multiplexers are used to convert back and forth analoge and digital to record and monitor.

Recording Media Specifications

Various cameras are available for recording the video, such as fixed cameras, PTZ cameras (PAN, TILT, and ZOOM), night vision cameras, thermal recognition cameras, etc. Again, camera selection depends on the need for clarity, surrounding environment, gravity level of recording situation and, of course, budget.
